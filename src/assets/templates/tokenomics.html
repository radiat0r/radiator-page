Tokenomics

100 Mio. $RDT

Incentives & Rewards 25%

Participation in the ecosystem of RADIATOR & Partners is rewarded. E.g.: if you buy a cashback applicable product from partners you will receive not just $XRD cashback, but also a $RDT reward which depends on the amount of spent $XRD.

Incentives & Rewards Burn 25%

For every paid Incentive & Reward, a certain amount is being burned.

Airdrops & Sales 19%

Delegators receive regular airdrops for staking $XRD on the RADIATOR node. Read more here. In addition, there are unregular airdrops for special participation in the ecosystem of RADIATOR & Partners. A limited sale is considered.

Founder 16%

The RADIATOR founders have many years of experience in project management, customer relations, marketing, and sales and a combined experience of three decades in software development. The founder tokens are unlocked and distributed at a monthly interval of 700k $RDT.

Marketing & Development 10%

These funds are used for exchange listing fees, marketing cooperations, and commissioned work.

Liquidity 5%

5% of the RDT supply is used for liquidity on various exchanges. The second token (e.g. $XRD or $USDA), which is needed for paired liquidity pools, is paid from the RADIATOR B2B revenue and has no effect on the tokenomics of $RDT.
