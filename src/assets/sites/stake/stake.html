Stake with us

Our Radiator ("RADix validATOR") node is run by german Radix community members. The node runs on a 24/7 monitored dedicated bare metal server. (Intel Core i7-8700, 2x SSD M.2 NVMe 1 TB, 4x RAM 16384 MB DDR4, NIC 1 Gbit - Intel I219-LM) To optimize handling of updates, one node is always up and running. A backup node runs on a ESXi Cluster.

Rewards

Not only will you receive passive income by staking on the Radiator node ($XRD token with ~8% APY), but also you will receive our $RDT cashback utility token via frequent airdrops. The more $XRD you stake, the more $RDT you will receive. But everybody will receive a fair share.

Conditions

To ensure everybody receives a fair share at some point, the airdrop parameters will change frequently and without prior notice between:

  • staking duration
  • min. stake
  • max. stake
  • min. airdrop amount per wallet
  • max. airdrop amount per wallet
